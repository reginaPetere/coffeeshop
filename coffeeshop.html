Espresso-Based Coffees

ESPRESSO

A strong and concentrated coffee made by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans

MACCHIATO

"stained" with a small amount of steamed milk, resulting in a bold flavor with a hint of creaminess.

CORTADO

Equal parts espresso and steamed milk, creating a balanced and smooth drink.

FLAT WHITE

A velvety espresso-based drink with steamed milk, known for its smooth texture and strong coffee flavor.

CAPPUCCINO

Equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam, offering a creamy and frothy experience.

LATTE

Espresso with a generous amount of steamed milk and a small layer of foam, delivering a creamy and mild coffee flavor.

MOCHA

Espresso combined with chocolate syrup or cocoa powder, topped with steamed milk and whipped cream, offering a rich and indulgent coffee-chocolate flavor.

AMERICANO

Espresso diluted with hot water, creating a milder and larger beverage while preserving the intensity of espresso.

RED EYE

A regular brewed coffee with a shot of espresso added, providing an extra caffeine boost and a richer flavor profile.

AFFOGATO

A dessert consisting of a scoop of vanilla ice cream "drowned" with a shot of hot espresso.

Brewed Coffees:


DRIP COFFEE (Regular)

Coffee brewed by passing hot water through ground coffee beans using a drip coffee maker, offering a classic and familiar coffee taste.

DRIP COFFEE (Decaf)

Decaffeinated coffee brewed using the same method as regular drip coffee, providing a caffeine-free option without sacrificing flavor.

FRENCH PRESS

uCoffee brewed by steeping coarse coffee grounds in hot water and then pressing them down with a plunger, resulting in a full-bodied and richly flavored beverage.

POUR-OVER

Coffee brewed by manually pouring hot water over coffee grounds in a filter, offering a clean and nuanced flavor profile with control over brewing variables.

AEROPRESS

Coffee brewed by pressing hot water through coffee grounds using air pressure, resulting in a smooth and flavorful cup with a quick brewing process.

CHEMEX COFFEE

Coffee brewed using a Chemex coffee maker, which involves pouring hot water over coffee grounds in a special filter, producing a clean and crisp flavor profile.

SIPHON COFFEE

Coffee brewed using a siphon coffee maker, where water is heated in a lower chamber and forced into an upper chamber with coffee grounds, offering a unique and theatrical brewing experience with a clean and aromatic taste.

TURKISH COFFEE

Coffee brewed by boiling finely-ground coffee beans with water and sugar in a special pot called a cezve, resulting in a strong and thick beverage with a rich foam on top.

COLD BREW

Coffee brewed using cold water over an extended period, typically 12-24 hours, resulting in a smooth and mellow beverage with low acidity and a subtle sweetness.

ICED COFFEE

Regular brewed coffee served over ice, offering a refreshing and chilled alternative to hot coffee with the same familiar flavors.
